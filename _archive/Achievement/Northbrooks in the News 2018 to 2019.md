<p><strong><u>National Orienteering Race (Schools)</u></strong>
</p>
<p><strong>14 July 2018</strong>
</p>
<p>During the recently concluded National Orienteering Race (Schools) (14
Jul 2018) at Singapore Polytechnic, our students have emerged as&nbsp;<strong>CHAMPIONS</strong>&nbsp;in
BOTH the pair (B Div Boys) and relay category (Sec Sch Girls).&nbsp; The
students displayed Grit and Resilience throughout this competition.</p>

<p><strong><u>Malaysia Orienteering Asia Cup (Perak, Malaysia)</u></strong>
</p>
<p><strong>16 May 2018</strong>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:270px;height:340px;margin-left:15px;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/SchAchievement.jpeg">

<p>12 Brooksians were selected to represent our school at the Malaysia Orienteering
Asia Cup in Perak, Malaysia between 11 to 14 May 2018.&nbsp;These students
were selected based on their orienteering and athletic abilities.&nbsp;The
event is an international World Ranking Event (WRE) which attracted orienteering
athletes from around the world. Our Brooksians displayed great grit and
resilience by enduring and overcoming challenging terrain and tricky checkpoints.
Our Orienteering team has achieved great success at the event.

<p><strong><u>Annual Greendale Adventure Race</u></strong>
</p>
<p><strong>On 14 Apr 2018</strong>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:70%" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Greendale.jpg">
</div>
<p>The school's Orienteering Team competed in the Annual Greendale Adventure
Race. The students ran, cycled and navigated around Punggol for three hours
with surprise checkpoints testing them in tent pitching, fire making and
First Aid.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Two of our teams emerged&nbsp;<strong>CHAMPION</strong>&nbsp;in the boys'
