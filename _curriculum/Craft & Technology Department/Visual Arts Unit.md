<p>The Visual Arts Unit aims to enable every Brooksian to be&nbsp;<strong>visually literate</strong>&nbsp;and
to&nbsp;<strong>appreciate art</strong>.&nbsp;Brooksians studying Visual
Art will be equipped with skills to communicate their ideas by creating
visuals using different art forms and mediums to see the value and relevance
of art in their lives.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/visualarts.gif">
</div>
<h4><strong>Visually Literate</strong></h4>
<p>Brooksians will be able to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Observe, perceive and interpret (make meaning to what they see)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Visually communicate and express their experiences/ideas through art-making</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Appreciate Art</strong></h4>
<p>Brooksians will be able to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>See the value and recognise the relevance of art in their lives.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Articulate their interpretation of artworks.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Annual Art &amp; Design Exhibition</strong></h2>
<p>Brooksians participate in our annual Art &amp; Design exhibition held
at the end of the school year to celebrate their outstanding achievements
in Art and D&amp;T works to instill a sense of pride and to encourage the
appreciation of art in Brooksians. The exhibition is open to all staff
to participate in the exhibition.&nbsp;The event is open to all Northbrooks
stakeholders, staff and Brooksians.</p>
<p>Please visit our latest site here:&nbsp;<a href="https://tinyurl.com/46f2trts" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://tinyurl.com/46f2trts</a>
</p>
<p>Within that site is also a&nbsp;<strong>3D Virtual Exhibition</strong>&nbsp;featuring
the special section "<strong><em>@rt My Own Time - No Pressure...</em></strong>"
where students are recognized for their Art creation beyond&nbsp;the curriculum
and pertaining to their own interests.</p>
