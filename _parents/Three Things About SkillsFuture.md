<h4>1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; What is SkillsFuture?</h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/SkillsFuture.png">
</div>
<p>Watch this <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rdBobyZLl7M" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">video</a> to
find out more about SkillsFuture:</p>
<p>One key feature of Budget 2015 is SkillsFuture – a national movement that
aims to provide opportunities for every citizen to develop their fullest
potential throughout life, regardless of their starting points. It will
promote skills mastery in every job, and foster a culture of lifelong learning
for all Singaporeans.</p>
