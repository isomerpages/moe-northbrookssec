<p><strong><u>National Orienteering Race (Schools)</u></strong>
</p>
<p><strong>On 14 July 2018</strong>

students displayed Grit and Resilience throughout this competition.</p>

<p><strong><u>Malaysia Orienteering Asia Cup (Perak, Malaysia)</u></strong>
</p>
<p><strong>On 16 May 2018</strong>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">

Orienteering Asia Cup @ Perak, Malaysia between 11 to 14 May 2018.&nbsp;These

athletes from around the world were present. Our Brooksians displayed great
grit and resilience by enduring and overcoming challenging terrain and
tricky checkpoints. Our Orienteering team has achieved great success at
the event.

<p><strong><u>Annual Greendale Adventure Race</u></strong>
</p>
<p><strong>On 14 Apr 2018</strong>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:70%" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Greendale.jpg">
</div>
<p>The school Orienteering Team competed in the Annual Greendale Adventure
Race. The students ran, cycled and navigated around Punggol for three hours
with surprise checkpoints testing them in tent pitching, fire making and
First Aid.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Two teams have emerged as&nbsp;<strong>CHAMPIONS</strong>&nbsp;in the
boys' category and mixed category.
<br>The students displayed great resilience and grit to complete this race.</p>
